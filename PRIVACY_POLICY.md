# Privacy Policy for AsciiTab

AsciiTab does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

### Data Storage
All user data, including settings, tasks, notes, and preferences, is stored locally within the user's browser using standard web storage mechanisms (`localStorage` and `storage.local`). This data never leaves the user's device.

### Tracking and Analytics
AsciiTab does not use analytics, tracking scripts, or third-party services that collect user information. We do not monitor your browsing habits or search history.

### External Requests
External requests are limited to user-initiated actions:
1.  **Search Queries**: When you perform a search, you are redirected to the search engine of your choice.
2.  **Search Suggestions**: If enabled, the extension fetches potential search terms from public APIs (e.g., Google Suggestions). No personal identifiers are sent with these requests.

### Data Sharing
No personal information is shared, sold, or transmitted to any external parties.

By using AsciiTab, you agree to this privacy policy.
